How you can find us

Talk to our friendly and experienced team on the phone – (03) 5480 7555 or Freecall 1800 804 446 (within Australia) or call into our wonderful heritage listed Information Centre at 2 Heygarth Street Echuca. (On the left just before you cross over the bridge from Echuca to Moama.)

Murray River Cruises in Echuca-Moama

Paddlesteamers have one hour cruises along the Murray River leaving from the Port of Echuca Wharf, Riverboat Dock or the Onion Patch, from 10.00am until about 3.30pm daily. Or perhaps you would like to take a longer leisurely luncheon or dinner cruise. You can even travel by paddlesteamer to a winery where you can have lunch, taste some fine wines and be collected the the paddlesteamer later in the afternoon.
Overnight may be more your style, with one, two and three night cruises are available from Echuca-Moama. Enjoy the river from sunrise until sunset, this is truly a remarkable experience. Why not captain a boat yourself? To hire a houseboat all you need is a valid drivers license to navigate the river at your own pace. Eco-tourism cruises are also available from the Barmah, (20minute drive from Echuca-Moama) venturing through the Barmah Wetlands and the Narrows.
